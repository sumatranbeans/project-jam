## How to Play

### Round Flow (90 seconds)

1. **Encoder Sees Prompt (5 sec)**
   - One player (Encoder) sees a phrase/title
   - Category is shown to everyone

2. **Emoji Encoding (30 sec)**
   - Encoder selects emojis to represent the phrase
   - Max 8 emojis allowed
   - Must be at least 2 emojis
   - Can't use letter/number emojis (ğŸ”¤, 1ï¸âƒ£, etc.)

3. **Guessing Phase (45 sec)**
   - Emoji sequence appears on TV
   - All other players race to guess
   - First correct guess wins
   - Multiple guesses allowed

4. **Reveal & Scoring**
   - If guessed: Guesser +2, Encoder +2
   - If nobody guesses: Encoder 0, everyone else +1

### Rotation
- Encoder role rotates each round
- Everyone gets equal turns encoding
