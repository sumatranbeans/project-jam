## How to Play

### Setup (10 seconds)
1. Players join via QR code
2. Choose difficulty (affects timer speed and category complexity)
3. Everyone starts with 3 lives (â¤ï¸â¤ï¸â¤ï¸)

### Gameplay
1. **First Word**: Game shows a starting word and category
2. **Chain Building**:
   - Players take turns clockwise
   - Each word must START with the LAST LETTER of the previous word
   - Word must fit the current category
   - You have limited time (starts at 8 seconds, gets faster)

3. **Category Shifts**: Every 3-5 words, the category changes!
   - TV announces: "CATEGORY CHANGE! ğŸ¯ Now: PLACES!"
   - Player must adapt mid-chain

4. **The Avalanche**:
   - Visual avalanche creeps down the screen
   - Timer gets shorter each round (8â†’7â†’6â†’5â†’4â†’3 seconds)
   - If you fail to answer, you lose a life
   - Word already used? Lose a life!

5. **Elimination**: Last player standing wins!

## What You'll Learn

### Vocabulary Building
- Expands word knowledge across categories
- Reinforces spelling (last letter awareness)
- Introduces new words when others play them

### Cognitive Skills
- **Processing speed**: Quick word retrieval under pressure
- **Category switching**: Mental flexibility when topics change
- **Pattern recognition**: Remembering which letters are easy/hard

### Social Skills
- Graceful handling of mistakes (everyone messes up!)
- Cheering others on while waiting
- Learning new words from other players
