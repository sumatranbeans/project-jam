## How to Play

### Game Modes

**Mode A: Partnership Pairs**
- Fixed pairs compete against other pairs
- Same two people try to meld each round
- Best for 4-6 players

**Mode B: Rotating Partners**
- Partners change each round
- Everyone pairs with everyone eventually
- Best for 3-5 players

### Round Flow (45 seconds)

1. **Pair Selection (5 sec)**
   - Two players are highlighted as the current pair
   - They must try to answer the same

2. **Prompt Shown (5 sec)**
   - A question appears to everyone
   - Example: "Name a farm animal"

3. **Silent Thinking (10 sec)**
   - Both players type their answer privately
   - NO COMMUNICATION allowed
   - No talking, gesturing, or hints

4. **Lock In**
   - Both must submit before time runs out
   - Auto-submit at deadline if not locked

5. **The Reveal**
   - Both answers shown simultaneously
   - MELD = same answer = points!
   - Different = no points, but often hilarious

### Scoring
| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Perfect Meld (exact match) | +3 |
| Near Meld (very close) | +1 |
| No Meld | 0 |

## The Magic Moment

**The Impossible Meld**

Prompt: "Name something you'd bring to a deserted island"

Everyone expects: water, food, phone, boat...

Mom types: "hammock"
Jake (10) types: "hammock"

MIND MELD!

The room explodes. "HAMMOCK?! Why did you both say hammock?!"

Mom: "Because Jake always wants to nap in one!"
Jake: "Because Mom always says she wants to relax!"

They understood each other so well they synced on an unexpected answer. That's the magic.

## Strategy Tips

Shown on loading screens:

1. **Think obvious first**: What's the MOST common answer?
2. **Know your partner**: What would THEY think is obvious?
3. **Avoid overthinking**: First instinct is often right
4. **Build a pattern**: If you both keep saying "safe" answers, stick with it
5. **Learn from misses**: "Next time, let's both go classic"
